Playtime

  • Choose wood toys over plastic, especially ones that baby will put in mouth
  • Anything made by Lovevery
  • Choose silicon over plastic
  • Baby Gate - didn’t research this one too much. It’s a gate. Dean will probably try to eat it, but hopefully not too frequently. It’s metal and sold by Walmart at Walmart.com. Also sold by Target and Chewy. Bought: Regalo Wide Adjustable Baby Gate
  • Baby Playmat (for crawling and playrooms).
  • Options: PVC, EVA, PE, Cotton.
  • The Decision: We decided to buy a high quality EVA playmat. EVA is the best of both worlds. The primary risk of EVA is that formaldehyde is used in the creation, and low quality mats could contain more of that. People are going to be walking on your playmat. Something easy to clean, soft, and stable is better. Cotton gets dirty and we won’t clean it enough. EVA is not as “safe” as cotton, but low risk overall so the benefits outweigh the risks for us in this case.Research here!
